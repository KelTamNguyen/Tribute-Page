Todd In The Shadows

Influential Music Youtuber/Critic

Here's a time line of Todd In The Shadows' life:

  • 1984 - Born on June 3rd
  • 2006 - Creates Youtube account
  • 2007 - A broken car antenna forces him to listen to pop music on the radio and eventually get into pop music
  • 2010 - Joins That Guy With The Glasses (TGWTG) (known today as Channel Awesome)
  • 2012 - Releases the first episode of One Hit Wonderland, a series of retrospectives looking at "one-hit wonders" of the past
  • 2014 - Starts Cinemadonna, a film-by-film retrrospective of Madonna's acting career
  • 2017 - Kicks off Trainwreckords, a documentary/review series focused on career-reviewing albums from various artists
  • 2018 - Parts Ways with Channel Awesome
  • 2020 - Gets interviewed by Billboard magazine
